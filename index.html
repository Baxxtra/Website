About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher and Lecturer at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), with a research focus on sustainable and climate finance. My work explores how financial markets assess corporate climate and biodiversity risks, and I use text analysis, machine learning, and financial econometrics to study risk pricing. In 2024, I was awarded the ECB Lamfalussy Fellowship for my research on biodiversity risk in syndicated lending.

Research Interests & Publications

Research Interests:
